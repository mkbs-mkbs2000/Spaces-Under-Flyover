Spaces Under Flyovers

How Other Places Have Done It

Hover over the points on the map and click on them to learn more about the five examples of revitalisation of underutilised spaces under flyovers. To look at other locations, click on the button below to return to the default extent of the map, and click on a new point on the map.

Claiborne Corridor is located under the Interstate-10 Expressway, along North Claiborne Avenue between St Bernard Avenue and Canal Street, in New Orleans, Louisiana, USA.

Bentway is located under the Gardiner Expressway, between Fort York Boulevard and Fort York Historic National Site, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

One Green Mile is located under the Elphinstone Flyover, along Senapati Bapat Marg, in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

A8ernA is located under the A8 motorway, between N203 provincial road and the Zaan River, in Zaanstad, North Holland, the Netherlands.

Taman Film is located under the Jalan Layang Pasupati flyover, in Bandung, West Java, Indonesia.
